Skyrim

This single player rpg takes you back in time to a fantasy land known as Tamriel.

Syrim is a cold, and snowy country in Tamriel. This allows for many monsters spawned from the frost to attack your character. Don't worry. Your character is not an ordinary person, your character is the Dragonborne. Your mission is to learn how to use your voice to stop the dragons from returning. Yes, dragons are coming back as soon as so start the game.

Aside from the main story, this rpg allows for the player to go off and do whatever they want.

  • Brew potions
  • Build a house
  • Smith armor and weapons
  • Explore dungeons
  • Have a family
  • Fight monsters
  • Put an end to the civil war in Skyrim

The only thing stopping you from doing something is your imagination, and maybe the bugs.

This game is so rich that people still have a great time playing it a decade after its release.

So what is stopping you?
